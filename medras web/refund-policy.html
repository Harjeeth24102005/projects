Refund Policy

Our transparent refund policy ensures fair treatment for all our patients. Learn about our refund procedures for services and medications.

Last Updated: January 2023

Introduction

At Medras Clinic, we strive to provide the highest quality healthcare services. We understand that situations may arise where refunds are necessary. This policy outlines our procedures for processing refunds for various services and products.

Our refund policy is designed to be fair, transparent, and compliant with all applicable regulations governing healthcare services in India.

General Refund Principles

We follow these general principles for all refund requests:

  • Fair Treatment: All refund requests are evaluated fairly and consistently
  • Timely Processing: Refunds are processed within 7-14 working days
  • Original Payment Method: Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Documentation Required: Proper documentation must be provided for all refund requests
  • Service Charges: Administrative/service charges may apply in certain cases

Pharmacy Refunds

Eligible for Refund

Unopened medicines with intact packaging and valid receipt, returned within 7 days of purchase

Conditional Refund

Medicines with manufacturing defects or expired before expiry date (subject to verification)

Not Eligible

Opened medicines, refrigerated items, or prescription medicines unless defective

Pharmacy Refund Process

1

Request Submission

Submit refund request at pharmacy counter with original receipt and unused medicines within 7 days

2

Verification

Our pharmacist will verify the condition of medicines and check purchase records (1-2 working days)

3

Processing

Approved refunds are processed through original payment method (7-10 working days)

Consultation Refunds

Important Notice

Consultation fees are generally non-refundable once the doctor has provided services. However, exceptions may be made under specific circumstances.

Refund eligibility for consultations:

  • Before Consultation: 100% refund if cancelled at least 4 hours before appointment
  • Doctor Unavailability: 100% refund if doctor is unavailable for scheduled appointment
  • Clinic Cancellation: Full refund if clinic cancels the appointment
  • During Consultation: No refund once consultation has started
  • No Show: No refund for missed appointments without prior cancellation

Cancellation Charges:

  • Cancellation 4-24 hours before appointment: 50% refund
  • Cancellation less than 4 hours before appointment: No refund
  • Emergency cancellations (with medical certificate): 100% refund

Diagnostic Test Refunds

Refund policies for diagnostic tests vary based on the type of test and stage of service:

Before Sample Collection

100% refund if cancelled at least 24 hours before scheduled test

After Sample Collection

No refund once sample has been collected, except in cases of test failure

Test Delays

Partial refund or complimentary retest if results are delayed beyond promised timeframe

Package Tests: For health checkup packages, refunds are calculated based on tests already completed. Unused tests are refunded at package rate.

Emergency Services

Emergency service charges are generally non-refundable as resources are allocated immediately for critical care. However:

  • Triage Assessment: If patient is assessed and referred without treatment, consultation fee may be refunded
  • Service Not Provided: If emergency team arrives but services are not required/used, basic charges may apply
  • Ambulance Services: Cancelled ambulance requests are subject to cancellation charges based on distance travelled

How to Request a Refund

Refund Request Process

📝

Step 1: Submit Request

Visit our billing counter or email refunds@medrasclinic.com with your request details

📄

Step 2: Provide Documents

Submit original receipt, identification proof, and any supporting documents

⏳

Step 3: Processing Time

Refunds are processed within 7-14 working days from approval date

✅

Step 4: Confirmation

You will receive confirmation email/SMS once refund is processed

Required Documents:

  • Original receipt/invoice
  • Valid photo identification
  • Bank details (for electronic transfers)
  • Original credit/debit card (for card refunds)
  • Supporting medical documents (if applicable)

Important Notes

  • All refunds are processed in Indian Rupees (INR)
  • Foreign currency transactions are refunded at the exchange rate on refund processing date
  • Bank charges for electronic transfers are borne by the patient
  • Cash refunds above ₹10,000 require additional verification
  • Refund requests must be made within 30 days of service/transaction
  • This policy is subject to change without prior notice
  • In case of disputes, the decision of Medras Clinic management is final

Fraud Prevention

We take refund fraud seriously. Any attempt to obtain refunds through false claims or misrepresentation will result in legal action and permanent ban from our services.

Policy Updates

This refund policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our services or regulatory requirements. The updated policy will be effective immediately upon posting on our website.
