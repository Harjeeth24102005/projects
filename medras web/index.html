Pharmacy Services

Our full-service pharmacy operates round the clock to ensure you have access to medicines whenever you need them. We maintain a comprehensive inventory of prescription and over-the-counter medications.

Our experienced pharmacists are available for consultation to help you understand your medications, potential side effects, and proper usage. We also provide medication reviews and compliance packaging.

Special services include generic medicine options that can save you up to 80% on prescription costs, home delivery for convenience, and automatic refill reminders.

Medical Clinic

Our clinic is staffed by experienced and qualified doctors across various specialties. We provide comprehensive medical care from routine checkups to management of chronic conditions.

Services include general physician consultations, specialist referrals, minor surgical procedures, vaccination services, and health checkup packages tailored to different age groups and needs.

We focus on preventive healthcare and offer regular health monitoring, lifestyle counseling, and chronic disease management programs to help you maintain optimal health.

Book Consultation Health Checkup

Diagnostic Center

Our state-of-the-art diagnostic center is equipped with modern technology for accurate and timely diagnosis. We offer a comprehensive range of laboratory tests and imaging services.

Services include complete blood tests, digital X-ray, ultrasound, ECG, cardiac tests, advanced laboratory investigations, and specialized diagnostic procedures.

We prioritize accuracy and timely reporting with most reports available on the same day. Patients can also access their reports online through our secure patient portal.
