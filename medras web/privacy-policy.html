Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information at Medras Clinic.

Last Updated: January 2023

Introduction

At Medras Clinic, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our services.

We comply with all applicable data protection laws and regulations, including the Indian Medical Council Regulations and other relevant healthcare privacy standards.

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide you with quality healthcare services:

Personal Information

  • Basic Details: Name, date of birth, gender, contact information
  • Contact Information: Address, phone number, email address
  • Identification: Government ID numbers, photograph
  • Emergency Contact: Name and contact details of emergency contact person

Medical Information

  • Medical History: Past illnesses, surgeries, allergies
  • Current Health Status: Symptoms, diagnoses, treatment plans
  • Test Results: Laboratory, imaging, and diagnostic test results
  • Medication Details: Prescriptions, dosage, frequency
  • Insurance Information: Policy details, coverage information

Technical Information

  • Website Usage: IP address, browser type, pages visited
  • Cookies: Information stored on your device to enhance user experience
  • Appointment Data: Booking history, preferences, feedback

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Healthcare Services: To provide medical diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up care
  • Appointment Management: To schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments
  • Billing and Insurance: To process payments and insurance claims
  • Communication: To send appointment reminders, test results, and health information
  • Quality Improvement: To enhance our services and patient care standards
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Emergency Situations: To contact emergency services or family members when necessary

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can view your medical records
  • Secure Storage: Physical and electronic security measures protect data storage
  • Regular Audits: Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Staff Training: All staff undergo regular privacy and security training
  • Incident Response: Established procedures for handling data breaches

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal and medical information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (where legally permissible)
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Receive your data in a portable format

Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Healthcare Providers: Doctors, specialists, and other medical professionals involved in your care
  • Insurance Companies: For processing claims and payments
  • Government Agencies: When required by law or for public health purposes
  • Service Providers: Third parties who assist in providing services (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Emergency Services: In life-threatening situations

Note: We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide healthcare services
  • Comply with legal requirements (medical records are typically retained for 5+ years as per regulations)
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

After the retention period, we securely dispose of your information using appropriate methods.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes through our website or direct communication. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
